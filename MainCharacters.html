Main characters:

Max Rockatansky:

Max Rockatansky is the title character and
antihero protagonist of the Australian post-apocalyptic
action film series Mad Max. Tom Hardy plays the character of Max in the movie.



Imperator Furiosa:

Imperator Furiosa is a fictional character in the 2015 film Mad Max: Fury Road. She is a war captain under Immortan Joe but turns against him in order to free "The Five Wives", Joe's female concubines. Charlize Theron plays fusriosa in the movie.
